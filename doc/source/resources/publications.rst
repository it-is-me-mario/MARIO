Projects
-----------------

.. raw:: html

   <div class="research-project-heading">
     <img src="../_static/images/logo_entice.svg" alt="ENTICE logo" class="research-project-logo" />
   </div>

ENTICE (Horizon Europe ID 101184775) studies how trade and trade
policy interact with climate, industry, and environmental outcomes. It builds
new data and modelling capacity to assess these links with greater sectoral
detail, broader environmental scope, and stronger policy relevance.
`Link <https://doi.org/10.3030/101184775>`_



.. raw:: html

   <div class="research-project-heading">
     <img src="../_static/images/logo_iam_compact.png" alt="IAM COMPACT logo" class="research-project-logo" />
   </div>

IAM COMPACT (Horizon Europe ID 101056306) focused on expanding
integrated assessment modelling for climate policy design beyond narrow
technology pathways. The project combined model ensembles with co-creation,
transparency, and broader social and political dimensions of climate action.
`Link <https://doi.org/10.3030/101056306>`_


.. raw:: html

   <div class="research-project-heading">
     <img src="../_static/images/logo_fulfill.png" alt="FULFILL logo" class="research-project-logo" />
   </div>

FULFILL (Horizon 2020 ID 101003656)investigated how sufficiency
and lifestyle change can contribute to deep decarbonisation. It combined
empirical fieldwork, systemic assessment, and citizen-oriented policy work to
understand barriers, enablers, and impacts of sufficiency-oriented lifestyles.
`Link <https://doi.org/10.3030/101003656>`_

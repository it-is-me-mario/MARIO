How-to Guides
=============

These pages are task-oriented. Use them when you already know what you want to
do and need the shortest route to a working solution.

The how-to guides deliberately avoid repeating the full conceptual background.
Instead, they focus on concrete tasks such as parsing a file, exporting a
database, aggregating sets or diagnosing a failing workflow.
